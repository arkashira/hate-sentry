# 🚀 hate-sentry
**Power developers with AI-driven hate speech detection.** hate-sentry is an AI-powered content moderation tool that accurately identifies implicit and nuanced forms of hate speech, reducing false positives and negatives.

## Why hate-sentry?
- **Accurate Detection**: Identify hate speech with high accuracy, reducing false positives and negatives.
- **Nuanced Understanding**: Detect implicit and nuanced forms of hate speech, going beyond simple keyword matching.
- **Customizable**: Adapt to specific use cases and industries, ensuring effective content moderation.
- **Scalable**: Handle large volumes of content, making it suitable for large-scale applications.
- **Efficient**: Reduce manual review time and effort, increasing productivity and efficiency.
- **Reliable**: Provide consistent and reliable results, ensuring trust and confidence in the tool.
- **Transparent**: Offer insights into detection decisions, enabling further improvement and refinement.
