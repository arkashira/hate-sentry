
# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
- **Primary**: The most accurate AI-powered content moderation tool for nuanced hate speech, reducing false positives and negatives in social media and online communities.
- **Alternatives**:
  1. Best for social media platforms needing real-time, low-false-positive moderation.
  2. Ideal for enterprise communities requiring compliance with hate speech regulations.
  3. Solutions for online forums with high user-generated content and need for nuanced detection.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Personas)
1. **Social Media Manager** (e.g., Sarah Chen, 28, Head of Community at "TikTok for Professionals")  
   - Daily job: Monitor comments, respond to reports, ensure community guidelines are followed.  
   - Budget: $5k/month for moderation tools.  
2. **Community Manager** (e.g., Mike Rodriguez, 35, Lead at "GamerHub")  
   - Daily job: Handle user disputes, enforce rules, maintain positive community.  
   - Budget: $3k/month.  
3. **Enterprise HR Compliance Officer** (e.g., Lisa Wang, 42, Director at "TechCorp")  
   - Daily job: Ensure workplace communications comply with EEOC regulations, audit internal platforms.  
   - Budget: $20k/month.

## Channels (Top 3 with CAC estimate)
1. **LinkedIn Ads** (target: "Tech Lead", "Compliance Manager", "Community Manager" in social media, gaming, enterprise industries)  
   - CAC: $500  
2. **Content Marketing (Blog + SEO)** (target keywords: "hate speech detection AI", "false positive reduction", "content moderation tools")  
   - CAC: $200  
3. **Direct Sales Outreach** (target key accounts: large social media platforms, enterprise HR software vendors)  
   - CAC: $800  

## Content Cadence (Week-by-Week)
- **Week 1**: Blog post "The Hidden Costs of False Positives in Content Moderation"  
- **Week 2**: Whitepaper "Quantifying the Impact of Nuanced Hate Speech Detection"  
- **Week 3**: Case study (hypothetical) "How [Fictional Company] Reduced 40% of False Flags with Hate-Sentry"  
- **Week 4**: Webinar "AI in Moderation: Balancing Free Speech and Safety"  
- **Week 5**: Email newsletter with stats on hate speech trends  
- **Week 6**: Guest post on industry blog (e.g., "TechCrunch")  
- **Week 7**: Video demo of tool in action  
- **Week 8**: Customer testimonial video  
- **Week 9**: Whitepaper on compliance regulations (e.g., EU's Digital Services Act)  
- **Week 10**: Webinar on new feature (if any)  
- **Week 11**: Email campaign to ICPs with case study  
- **Week 12**: Launch event (virtual or in-person)  

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30**: Beta launch with 10 select partners (small social media platforms, gaming forums)  
- **D-0**: Official launch with pricing ($199/month for small teams, $999/month for enterprise)  
- **D+30**: 100 paying customers, 50k DAU, 80% customer satisfaction  
- **D+90**: 500 paying customers, $1M MRR
