# Customer Journey Map

## Aware
**Trigger Event:** User experiences or witnesses hate speech on a platform they use or manage.
**Friction Points:** Lack of awareness of advanced moderation tools, reliance on basic keyword filters.
**User Emotions:** Frustration, concern, helplessness.
**Opportunities to Delight:** Educational content on the impact of hate speech and the need for advanced moderation.
**Metric:** Number of users who visit the product website or landing page.

## Consider
**Trigger Event:** User searches for solutions to improve content moderation.
**Friction Points:** Overwhelming number of options, skepticism about new tools, concerns about cost and effectiveness.
**User Emotions:** Overwhelmed, skeptical, hopeful.
**Opportunities to Delight:** Clear, concise product demos, case studies, and testimonials from similar platforms.
**Metric:** Number of users who sign up for a demo or request more information.

## Try
**Trigger Event:** User signs up for a free trial or demo of Hate-Sentry.
**Friction Points:** Complex onboarding process, technical issues, unclear instructions.
**User Emotions:** Excited, anxious, impatient.
**Opportunities to Delight:** Seamless onboarding experience, personalized support, quick setup.
**Metric:** Number of users who complete the onboarding process and start using the tool.

## Adopt
**Trigger Event:** User decides to purchase and implement Hate-Sentry.
**Friction Points:** Integration challenges, cost concerns, resistance from team members.
**User Emotions:** Confident, relieved, cautious.
**Opportunities to Delight:** Smooth integration process, dedicated customer support, regular updates and improvements.
**Metric:** Number of users who make a purchase and successfully integrate the tool into their platform.

## Expand
**Trigger Event:** User experiences positive results and seeks to expand usage.
**Friction Points:** Scaling challenges, additional costs, need for training.
**User Emotions:** Satisfied, ambitious, optimistic.
**Opportunities to Delight:** Scalable pricing plans, additional features, ongoing training and support.
**Metric:** Number of users who upgrade to higher plans or add additional features.
