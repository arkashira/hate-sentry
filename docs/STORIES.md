## Epic 1: Hate Speech Detection

### Story 1: Detect Hate Speech with High Accuracy
As a content moderator, I want hate-sentry to accurately identify hate speech with high accuracy, so that I can reduce false positives and negatives.

* Acceptance Criteria:
	+ Hate-sentry correctly identifies hate speech in 90% of cases.
	+ Hate-sentry correctly identifies non-hate speech in 95% of cases.
	+ Hate-sentry provides clear explanations for its detection decisions.
